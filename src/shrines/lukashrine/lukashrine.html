my history with luka

i'm going to out myself here, but i've actually had a really short history with luka.

when i initially came across luka, i didn't pay too much attention to her. at the time (~13ish) i was really into more higher pitched voices, such as miku and rin. it wasn't until a few years later (~15) when i listen to a song with meiko i started to get more attracted to lower pitched voices. even then, i still neglected to pay attention to luka, and focused on meiko and kaito heavily. i hadn't really listen to any songs of hers besides luka luka night fever, but it wasn't until late last year i started to take her more seriously, specifically when i heard stardust utopia. after that, i started seeking out more of her and became obsessed!

my favorite songs

stardust utopia - otetsu

one of the first songs other than luka luka night fever i had heard. this was the song that really solidified the fact that i love her ♥ i ADORE the way she sounds in this one, as well as the harmonies with a her higher pitched voice. the pv for this is also super cute as well.

double lariat - agoaniki

ALL TIME FAVORITE LUKA SONG. her tuning in this song is absolutely heavenly. she sounds so incredibly mature here and i love it. the choreography is also really well done and hypnotizing LOL. tako luka in the pv is just the cherry on top.
