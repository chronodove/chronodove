8/4

animal injury & death

19:20

a mourning dove was getting hunted by a falcon and ran into my window. she flew so hard she left an imprint.

she fell to the bottom of our porch, stunned by the impact and bleeding through her ear. her eye was also bloody. my brother and I accompanied her, trying to give her water, but she didn't drink. we layed her down in a sunny patch of grass and left her alone for a bit.

it seemed that she was getting a bit better, she started to fly and after i identified her she flew on to our roof.

i was worried that we would find her dead tomorrow but, maybe she'll be alright. or the falcon will come back to finish what it's started.

i can't imagine being her. so much pain she must be in. it's times like this i wish i didn't have a heart. why am i hurting for a bird so much?

still, the way her racing heart beat so hard that her body moved, and how even though she was prepared to die, she still flew. there's something meaningful in that.
