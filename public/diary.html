12:05

yesterday was very uneventful. i slept immediately after i came home from school. my mother and brother went to a diwali party next door, and he came home at eight - interrupting my sleep - and asked me to make him something to eat. i made him some food and tried to go back to sleep. i went to sleep around 22 and woke up at 3. i slept again at 6 and woke up to feed the birds at 7.

my younger cousin from india called me, i hadn't talked to her in over 2 years. we had a very nice conversation, though she cut out a lot, so i ended up just smiling and laughing most of the time. she made fun of how whitewashed i was. it didn't take it personally, she's not wrong. i don't speak hindi well, and i can only understand up to almost 4th standard level. she's otherwise very sweet. she is a lot like me. she's getting along very well with my father. i hope i get to go to india again and catch up.

after that i took another nap. i woke up at four. now i have been up since. i was very lazy yesterday. maybe i'll make it up today, or tommorow. who knows.

my mother and brother went to another party. they haven't come back yet at the time of writing. i helped her get ready. i just stared at her and came to the realization that she was my mother. i get that feeling often where i think, "wow, this woman gave birth to me. she isn't some woman i happen to live with right now." same thing happens with my father too. i have no idea why.

i found some very interesting sites today and followed them. i also tried to make a new button but it didn't work out. i'll try again later.
