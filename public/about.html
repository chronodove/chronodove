hello, my name is dove.

i'm a highschool student who is not very smart and is bad at math. nonetheless i still pursue math related fields like engineering & both data & computer science. my life expectancy is ~77 years.

i like to write, but i don't do it much. outside of that, i like to explore different websites, listen to vocaloid & alterative music, read, and sleep.

i absolutely adore birds, dogs, spiders, marine animals, monochrome colour schemes, 1am-4am, 24hr time (right next to 12hr time, ironic, didn't even intend on this), notebooks & legal notepads, stationary, & using "&" instead of writing out "and".

i can be an erratic person at times, especially if i've been off my medication for more than a week (you'll be able to tell from my journal entries, trust me). however, most of the time i'm very boring & annoying & unfunny. still, i hope you find some sort of mundane comfort looking at my site, like the sort of calm you (might) feel when you go people watching.

you can email me at chronodove[at]gmail.com. i will not respond immediately, especially if you are emailing me for the first time. so please don't think i am ignoring you, i am just mustering up the courage to reply. i'm not on a lot of social sites, though chances are if you see someone with the username chronodove, d0v3/e, or any variant of the two, it's most likely me.
